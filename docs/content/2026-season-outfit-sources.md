## Publication Rules

- Do not use the attached social-media composite images as product assets. They are references for layout only.
- Each published seed outfit has a dated ranking source, at least one brand/product source, and one original in-repo SVG asset source.
- The first production content pass should replace representative product category sources with dated official product pages or licensed supplier records before marketing copy claims "exact outfit".
- If an item cannot be verified to model level, keep the wording as "representative" in UI copy and metadata.
